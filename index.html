National Service Scheme
Under Technical Cell, Kerala Unit No : 225 & 302
Sree Chitra Thirunal College of Engineering, Trivandrum

Who are we?

National Service Scheme which is under the ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports is functioning in the campus with a volunteer strength of more than 200. Our NSS unit is now one among the most active NSS unit under NSS Technical Cell, Govt. of Kerala. The NSS Unit of this college was registered in 2009 (Reg. No: 225). Henceforth NSS have expanded both quantitatively and qualitatively over the years by undertaking various projects and programs in association with different Government departments and agencies. Now SCT College of Engineering has two NSS units (Unit no: 225 & 527).


What do we do?

NSS SCTCE engages in various activities like blood and organ donation camps and international youth exchange programs, embodying its motto. In recent times, NSS SCTCE’s prominent projects include 'Snehaveedu', an initiative for building homes for the underprivileged; ‘Prakrithika’, a project to build a full-fledged, Organic Vegetable Farm in Campus Premises; and along with these lifetime projects, there are also other important projects such as 'Koode,' 'Velicham,' ‘Sparsham' and more. As part of important campus proceedings, ‘Kismath’ is a fund-raising food fest that has become an annual tradition of the Units. The Camp Projects, ‘Punarjani’, and ‘Sneharamam’ received widespread recognition and acclaim among the larger NSS community. These initiatives highlight the unit's dedication to societal needs and personal development, earning widespread recognition and acclaim.
